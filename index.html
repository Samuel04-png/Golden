What is a Research Proposal?

A research proposal is a detailed plan outlining your intended research project. It demonstrates the significance of your research, methodology, and expected outcomes. Our expert writers help Zambian students craft compelling, well-structured proposals that meet academic standards.

Who Needs This Service?

  • Undergraduate students starting research projects
  • Postgraduate students (Masters level)
  • PhD candidates planning dissertations
  • Researchers applying for grants or funding

What We Need From You

Essential Information

  • Research topic or area of interest
  • Academic level (BSc, MSc, PhD)
  • Department/Faculty requirements
  • Word count or page limit
  • Submission deadline

Supporting Documents

  • University guidelines (if available)
  • Research interest notes
  • Preliminary literature (optional)
  • Supervisor feedback (if any)

Company Registration Services

Full company incorporation and compliance services in Zambia (PACRA, ZRA, NAPSA)

Start Your Business Correctly

Registering your company is the first step to doing business in Zambia. We handle the entire process with PACRA, ensuring your business is legally recognized and compliant from day one. We also assist with tax registration (TPIN) and other statutory requirements.

  • Digital Certificate of Incorporation
  • TPIN Registration (ZRA) included
  • Articles of Association drafting
  • Free Bank Account Opening Letter

Requirements

For Directors/Shareholders

  • Copies of NRC or Passport
  • Passport-sized photos (soft copy)
  • Physical & Postal Addresses
  • Phone numbers & Email addresses

Company Details

  • 3 Proposed Company Names (for search)
  • Nature of Business (Main activities)
  • Company Physical Address
  • Shareholding Structure (%)

Registration Process

Name Search & Clearance

We submit your proposed names to PACRA for clearance to ensure uniqueness.

Day 1

Document Preparation

Drafting of Articles of Association and completion of statutory registration forms (Form 2, 5, etc.).

Day 2

Submission & Processing

Formal submission to PACRA and payment of statutory fees. Monitoring of approval status.

Days 3-5

Certificate Issuance

Receipt of Digital Certificate of Incorporation and Share Certificates. ZRA TPIN registration.

Days 6-8

Compliance Note

Governing Body: Patents and Companies Registration Agency (PACRA)

All limited companies must file Annual Returns separately from tax returns to maintain legal status.

Note: A Company Secretary is required for all Limited Companies in Zambia.
